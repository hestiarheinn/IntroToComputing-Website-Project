Maximize Every Moment: Live Fully

I am Hestia Rhein A. Aguirre, an individual driven by curiosity and a hunger for continuous learning. Born and raised in Kabacan, North Cotabato. I am 19 years old and currently studying Bachelor of Science in Information Systems at the University of Southern Mindanao. I believe in the power of focus, striving to approach each challenge with an open mind and a determination to grow. Beyond my professional endeavors, I find solace in creating arts using crafts, which serve as a creative outlet and a source of rejuvenation.

I prioritize empathy and connection in every facet of my life. As an individual who values hard work, I strive to create meaningful relationships and foster environments where understanding and collaboration thrive.

My hobbies

Engaging in a colorful tapestry of hobbies brings immense joy and fulfillment to my life. With a paintbrush in hand, I explore the boundless realms of creativity on canvas, translating emotions into vibrant artworks that tell stories only colors can convey. On the volleyball court, the rhythm of spikes and dives becomes a dance of teamwork and adrenaline, fostering not only physical fitness but also a sense of camaraderie. In quieter moments, the gentle repetition of crochet hooks weaves a calming tapestry of intricate patterns, transforming yarn into cozy creations. Each hobby, be it painting, volleyball, or crocheting, serves as a unique avenue for self-expression and a delightful escape into worlds of imagination and skill.
